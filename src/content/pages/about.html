About Gorbua Thai Massage

Founded by Noi, Gorbua Thai Massage brings authentic Thai healing traditions to the heart of Gosforth, Newcastle. Originally from Kamphaeng Phet, Thailand, Noi has dedicated over 16 years to mastering the art of massage therapy, both in Thailand and across England.

Since establishing her practice in Newcastle in 2009, Noi has built a reputation for combining traditional Thai techniques with modern therapeutic approaches. Her journey led to opening Gorbua Thai Massage, where she has been providing expert care from our Gosforth location for the past two years.

At the heart of our practice is a genuine commitment to healing and accessibility. Noi believes in making therapeutic massage available to everyone who needs it, offering professional care at reasonable rates. Each session begins with a thorough consultation to understand your specific needs and create a tailored treatment plan.

For those seeking therapeutic treatment, we take a careful, progressive approach. The first session focuses on gentle techniques to assess and address your condition, with subsequent sessions building in intensity as needed. Many clients see significant improvement after three weekly sessions of our specialised care.

We invite you to experience the benefits of authentic Thai massage therapy in our peaceful Gosforth treatment room. Whether you're seeking relief from injury, managing chronic pain, or simply looking to enhance your wellbeing, we're here to support your journey to better health.
